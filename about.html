about

background:

Raisah (she/her) is currently studying software development at Ada Developers Academy in Seattle, Washington. Ada Developers Academy is a year-long software development bootcamp dedicated to bringing gender diversity to the tech industry. Before attending Ada, Raisah worked as an Operations Manager, bringing her skills in data organization to small nonprofits. She loves creative problem solving and working on coding problems that make you want to tear your hair out. Raisah grew up in Seattle and looks forward to giving back to her community once she graduates from the program

interests:

When she's not coding, Raisah enjoys watching cooking videos, experimenting with gluten-free baking and walking around Seattle. She volunteers at API Chaya and enjoys working on visual design projects.
